Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

ATSPass ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Ranevaoli Create. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

When you use ATSPass, we temporarily process:

  • Resume content — the text extracted from your uploaded file, used only to generate your ATS score and optimized version.
  • Job description — the text you paste, used only to match keywords and optimize your resume.
  • Email address — only if you choose to receive your optimized resume via email.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not store your resume or job description after processing.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not require account creation or login.

How we use your data

Your resume and job description are sent to OpenAI's API solely to generate the optimized resume. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy. We do not store this data after your session ends.

Your email address is used only to deliver your optimized resume and to notify us of your purchase. We do not send marketing emails.

Data retention

We do not retain your resume content or job description. Email addresses used for delivery are not added to any mailing list.

Third-party services

  • OpenAI — AI processing of resume content
  • PayPal — payment processing
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions

Your rights

You can request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing us at hello@ranevaoli.site. We will respond within 48 hours.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@ranevaoli.site
